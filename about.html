About Biscuit

Biscuit is a free stop motion animation program created by the members of Bricks in Motion, a website devoted to the art of creating stop motion animation with LEGO bricks, commonly known as "brickfilms".

The name Biscuit was coined by Bricks in Motion member "Carousel" and stands for:
Bricks in Motion Image Sequencing and Capture Unit Initiative.

Please note that despite its name Biscuit is not currently associated with Bricks in Motion in any official capacity.
